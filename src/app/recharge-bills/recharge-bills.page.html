How to Make Prepaid Mobile Recharge Online?

Mobile phones are an intrinsic part of our lives and with association, so is online mobile recharge. Online recharge gives you the liberty to recharge your mobile phone number anytime and from anywhere - be it from your home, office, restaurant or vacation, all you need is an internet connection. When you think of easy online recharge options, MobiKwik is the best platform to turn to.

You can now carry out your prepaid online recharge and bill payments for any number, for your friends and family using MobiKwik. Online recharges can be done through Net Banking, Debit Card, Credit Card, Visa or Mastercard and MobiKwik wallet. HDFC, SBI, CITI & ICICI banks and a host of other banks support Net Banking online recharge with ease.

The process of online recharge is also as simple as it sounds. All you have to do is :-

  1. Enter the phone number you want to carry out the online recharge for.
  2. Select the option from Prepaid/ Postpaid
  3. Select your mobile operator
  4. Select the circle you belong to.
  5. Check the online recharge plans designed exclusively for you or fill in the amount you want to recharge
  6. Enter your mobile number again and put the received OTP
  7. Make the online recharge payment and Voila!

Your online mobile recharge is all done.
